Operating rule suggested by Jots

For decor bookings, do not mark the job as confirmed until event date, exact venue, guest count, selected package, setup time, and deposit proof are attached.

Prescriptive analysis
  • Stuck: Quote cannot be final until the month and venue address are confirmed.
  • Owed: Deposit terms need confirming before this can become a booking.
  • Missing: Package choice and setup time are needed for staff handover.
  • Improve: Add a standard decor intake checklist to reduce back-and-forth before quoting.

Every business has rules. They are usually stored in the owner’s head. Jots helps capture those rules and apply them to future work.

Jots suggests. You approve. The business memory gets better over time.

Deposit rule: A booking is not confirmed until deposit proof is attached.
Quote rule: A photography job needs date, venue, package, shoot time, and delivery deadline.
Payment rule: A job is not complete until the balance is marked paid.
Handover rule: Staff should see the job summary, customer notes, location, and outstanding requirements.
